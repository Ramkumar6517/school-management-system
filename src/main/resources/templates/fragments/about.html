About EduSphere

JRP School is a unique co-educational English medium school established on 02 April, 2013 Tuesday at 01:30 pm affiliated to CBSE Board, New Delhi from class nursery to X located in calm, pleasant and pollution free atmosphere in rural area.
JRPS is a humble beginning in the enormous field of education with a mission of generating discipline, leadership and team spirit qualities, developing confidence, rising the internal activity and above all love & cooperation for fellow human beings. The school realize its mission through the guidance of advisory board and management.
JRPS believes in the concept of "Education helping, brightening the future". Education is an essential tool for bright future for all of us. We can achieve anything good in the life using the tool of education.
